Who We Are
Founded in 2010 by Attorney A. W. Roy and Ale M. Lynn, Ale & Roy PLLC focuses its practice on representing property owners who have suffered damages to their homes and businesses.
Our primary goal is to provide our clients with zealous representation to ensure they achieve the results and compensation they deserve.
With over 10 years of experience, our firm has been serving clients throughout New York.
Our primary goal is to provide our clients with zealous representation to ensure they achieve the results and compensation they deserve. As a boutique practice, Ale & Roy PLLC takes pride in offering personalized attention and responsive service to our clients.
We prioritize quality over quantity and limit our caseload to guarantee the most effective representation possible.
Our approach is comprehensive and unwavering, supported by a seasoned team of litigation attorneys and professional staff dedicated to litigation support.

What We Do

Property Insurance Claims

Was your property insurance claim denied? Delayed? Underpaid?
An insurance policy is a contract. As a policyholder, you paid a premium. In exchange for that payment, your insurance company made some promises to you.
If you feel your insurance company isn’t holding up their end of the bargain, please contact us for a free consultation.
Ale & Roy PLLC proudly represents policyholders in disputes with their property insurance companies.

Type of claims we handle:

  • Lightning
  • Theft/Burglary/Vandalism
  • Flood
  • Boiler and Machinery
  • Bad Faith

Insurance Fraud

The Firm has the depth of experience needed to manage the developments of an insurance fraud case from inception through prosecution to the fullest extent of the law in both civil and criminal courts.
The Ale & Roy PLLC Fraud Unit works with clients to determine which cases may qualify for prosecution and further develop these cases as an effective and solid case package to be submitted to the Division of Insurance Fraud, Offices of the State Attorney, or the United States Attorney’s Office.
The Ale & Roy PLLC Fraud Unit stays in constant communication with the Division of Insurance Fraud and prosecutors to provide on-going support and to keep all cases on track.
Moreover, as part of our overall Fraud Unit practices, Ale & Roy PLLC has extensive investigative capabilities.
Our capabilities draw on both traditional legal skills as well as extensive criminal investigative experience.
The firm’s efficient investigative practices are highly cost-effective and can reduce clients’ overall investigation costs.
